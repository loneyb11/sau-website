SA United Soccer Club

The mission of SA United Soccer Club is to provide a quality, safe and fun learning environment for all young players by employing only the best professional and dedicated coaches that are role models and display sound leadership skills. Our mission is to be the best grass roots club, offering programs for all players that want to learn, grow and develop a love of soccer. Using our core values of leadership, integrity, growth, respect, character, confidence, hard work and excellence, we will provide a creative environment that will instill a lifelong passion fo the game.

Growth

In all facets of development we strive for growth. Mental, physical, technical both on and off the field. Growth comes from success as well as facing adversity.

Character

It's who you are when you grow in the SA Utd Soccer Program.

Community

Community develops when a groups push in the right direction and strive for the best. Comunities become tighter when they grow together.

Respect

Our way is not the only way and we respect that others do things differently. Difference is what defines us. We just want our difference to be the one that followers seek.

Recreational Soccer

Due to the great success of the Rising Stars and Academy programs we felt that it was important to offer a recreational program. Not all players have the same ambitions and not all players start at the same time. The Recreational pragram gets the same love and attention the Academy and Competitve programs get... The rec program is geared towardw fostering that love of the game.
